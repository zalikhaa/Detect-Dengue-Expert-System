WAYS TO PREVENT DENGUE

We provide ways to prevent dengue as a precaution step for user to avoid getting dengue.

Hope this little information will be useful.




Mosquito repellents

Use a repellent with at least 10 percent concentration of diethyltoluamide (DEET), or a higher concentration for longer lengths of exposure. Avoid using DEET on young children.

Stagnant water

The mosquitoes which breed dengue thrive in stagnant water containing objects like tires, plastic covers, flower pots, petâ€™s water bowls, etc. Reducing the habitat available for these mosquitoes(by getting rid of stagnant water to breed) can help prevent dengue.

Close doors

Ensure that the windows are closed properly especially during dusk and dawn. This would eliminate the chances of any mosquitoes entering the house.
